Look Hear Collective Gallery

Dupont Underground Washington DC 9/27/19

The DuPont Underground presents a collaborative event by Look Hear Collective, four DC-based African American photographers whose work highlights the connection between the cultures of visual arts and music. The artists will speak briefly about the experience of capturing and creating the images on display, in conversation with event partners Washington Informer’s The Bridge. The evening will also include performances by local musicians and DJs, live mural painting by Katie Spak and pop-up fashion from DMV creatives. The photography exhibit will be on display at DuPont Underground from September 27-30th.
