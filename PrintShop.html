December 1st, 2018, Trill City focused its lens on a time period in underground music captured by photographer, Shedrick Pelt. A glimpse of his career has been curated in his recent book, "2009 And Beyond." Not only was his work available to view, additional context was offered by Texas rappers, Fat Tony of Houston's 3rd Ward, and Teeta of Austin's 24. These icons for the state reflected on their experiences with a conversation about their journeys, the culture of the time, and prospects about the future. The conversation followed up with a Q & A prompted by the audience and a performance by Shirt Off Fe. The events location was at Fine Southern Gentlemen's Austin location. #2009andbeyond
